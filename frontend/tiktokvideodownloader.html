how it works

Three steps, no accounts

1

Open the TikTok post

On the video you want, tap Share, then Copy link. Works with tiktok.com links and the short vm.tiktok.com share links.

2

Paste it above

The preview appears in about a second, with the video ready to save.

3

Download

The video saves straight to your device with a clean filename. No watermark, ever.

questions

Frequently asked questions

Do I get the TikTok video without the watermark?

Yes. SaveVid AI saves TikTok videos without the watermark, straight from the source, so you get a clean file with no overlay.

Is the TikTok downloader really free?

Yes. No popups, no redirects, no fake download buttons, ever. The code is open source, so you can verify that yourself or run your own copy.

Can I download TikTok photo slideshows?

Yes. Photo slideshows resolve to a photo grid: save any photo, save them all in one tap, or grab the soundtrack as an audio file. Your browser may ask once to allow multiple downloads.

Is it safe to use?

Yes. No login, no account, no tracking. Nothing is installed on your device, and the code is open source so you can read exactly what it does.

Who runs this?

SaveVid AI is an open source project by @israfill. Read the code, star it, or self-host it from the GitHub repository.
