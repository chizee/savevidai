how it works

Three steps, no accounts

1

Copy the post link

On the post with the video, tap Share, then Copy link. Works with x.com, twitter.com, and mirror links from Discord.

2

Paste it above

The preview appears in about a second, with every available quality and its file size listed.

3

Pick a size

The video saves straight from Twitter's CDN to your device with a clean filename. No watermark, ever.

questions

Frequently asked questions

Is SaveVid AI really free and ad-free?

Yes. No popups, no redirects, no fake download buttons, ever. The code is open source, so you can verify that yourself or run your own copy.

What quality do I get?

Every quality Twitter serves, up to the original upload resolution. All options are listed with file sizes; you choose.

Do private or age-restricted posts work?

No. SaveVid AI only works with public posts, and we would rather say that plainly than pretend otherwise.

Is downloading Twitter videos legal?

Personal use, like archiving your own posts, is generally fine. Reposting someone else's work without permission can infringe copyright. Use downloads responsibly.

Who runs this?

SaveVid AI is an open source project by @israfill. Read the code, star it, or self-host it from the GitHub repository.
